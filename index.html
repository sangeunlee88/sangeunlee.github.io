I am a Ph.D. candidate at Computer Vision Lab, Kyoto University, Japan, where I am advised by Prof. Ko Nishino. My research interests are 3D Vision and Robotics, especially I am currently focusing on extrinsic camera calibration using moving articulated objects such as humans and animals in wild, uncontrolled environments.
